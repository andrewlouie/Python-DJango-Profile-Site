Fees/Payment

Counselling and social work services are paid for either privately (out-of-pocket) or via your health insurance benefits. Counselling is not covered under OHIP however, my services are often reimbursable under extended health insurance plans (e.g., Greenshield, Sunlife) which cover the services of a Registered Social Worker. Payment is provided at the end of sessions and I provide you with a receipt which you submit to your health insurance provider for reimbursement. I would encourage you to call your benefits provider and ask if social work services are covered, how much you have available per year, and when your benefits renew.
