At Vitality Counselling Services I believe healing is possible and that negative life experiences do not have to go on controlling the future.

Vitality Counselling Services provides client-centred counselling services to children, adolescents, adults and families in the Kitchener/Waterloo area. Whether it is negotiating difficult life situations, dealing with anxiety (worry/fear), depression, anger, trauma, stress, relationships, parenting challenges, emotional management, low self-esteem, substance misuse, and other life related issues, I will adapt my approach to meet your personal goals and together we will work towards you achieving them.

The guidance I offer is balanced with my warm and genuine, yet direct style which often uses humour to help create a relaxed atmosphere. I believe strongly in the necessity of a therapeutic relationship and therefore I will strive to ensure that a trusting working relationship is developed - one that is based on mutual respect, openness and honesty.
